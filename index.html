How we do it

BEI curates investing competitions with large prizes. Our partner, Invest Org, curates investing courses alongside these competitions which our members can use to learn as they compete.

At BEI, we go beyond for our members by not only providing meaningful opportunities for our members but by partnering with other student-led organizations. Our partnership with Invest Org allows our members to complete investing courses while participating in our investing competitions but also trade in real-time on Invest Org’s proprietary trading platform.

At BEI our members curate content for FBLA and DECA competitive events; we also support member-led initiatives; by doing this we continue to expand our 75k+ student network.
